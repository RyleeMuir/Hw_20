Why whitewater rafting?

Whitewater rafting is a fun sport that all ages can be involved in. You have the freedom to choose what class (I-V) suits You experience level, and you can then practice and go from there.

How to start:

The best ways to get involved in rafting are below:

Find a community of people who raft

Take a local guiding course to learn the ropes

Start paying for trips with a local guide and get practical experience

Buy your river gear and make time to learn the skills
